Author Bios

Bridget Gelms is an Assistant Professor of English at San Francisco State University where she teaches courses in professional writing and rhetoric. She researches social media cultures, digital rhetorical practices, and online harassment. Her work has appeared in Present Tense: A Journal of Rhetoric in Society, enculturation, and The Writing Center Journal. You can find out more about her work at bridgetgelms.com or on Twitter @BridgetGelms.

Dustin Edwards is an Assistant Professor of Writing and Rhetoric at the University of Central Florida in Orlando. His work has appeared in Computers and Composition, enculturation, and Present Tense: A Journal of Rhetoric in Society, as well as in edited collections. His current work examines the material, environmental, and cultural damages of networked digitality in an era of Big Data. He can be reached via email at dustin.edwards@ucf.edu or Twitter @edwardsdusty.
